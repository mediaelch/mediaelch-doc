=====================================
Welcome to MediaElch's documentation!
=====================================

.. image:: images/MediaElch.png
   :align: center
   :alt: MediaElch Logo

MediaElch is a MediaManager for Kodi. Information about Movies, TV Shows,
Concerts and Music are stored as nfo files. Fanarts are downloaded automatically
from fanart.tv. Using the nfo generator, MediaElch can be used with other
MediaCenters as well.

*More in* :doc:`About MediaElch <about>`.
